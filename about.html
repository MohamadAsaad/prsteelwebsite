Prsteel Prosthetics & Orthotics

Prosthetics and Orthotics Components Manufacturer

Prsteel Prosthetics & Orthotics was founded in 2015 and has since focused on the production of high-quality lower limb prosthetic components. The company's products meet the needs of prosthetic providers around the world.

Prsteel produces a wide range of prosthetic components from titanium and stainless steel, including:


  • Knee joints
  • Locking mechanisms Life.
  • Silicone liners

Prsteel's products are known for their high quality and durability, helping people with limb loss live active and healthy lives.

Vision:

We are committed to providing prosthetic providers with high-quality components that meet their needs and help them provide the best possible care to patients.

Mission:

We are a leading manufacturer of prosthetic components, and we strive to provide innovative and high-quality products that meet the needs of our customers.

Values:

  • Quality: We are committed to producing high-quality products that meet the needs of our customers.
  • Innovation: We are constantly striving to develop new and innovative products.
  • Service: We are committed to providing outstanding customer service to our customers.

Achievements:

  • ISO 13485:2016 certified
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • CE certified
  • Products distributed in over 50 countries worldwide

Goals:

  • Expand into the global market
  • Develop new and innovative products
  • Enhance customer service
